About Our Academy

Assailant Fighters Academy was established in 2019 with a mission to develop young talented footballers and help them reach their full potential. Our state-of-the-art facilities and professional coaching staff create the perfect environment for player development.

We offer programs for all age groups and skill levels, from beginners to advanced players looking to pursue professional careers. Our holistic approach focuses on technical skills, tactical understanding, physical conditioning, and mental strength.

Our philosophy is centered around developing well-rounded athletes who excel not only on the field but also in life. We emphasize discipline, teamwork, sportsmanship, and academic excellence.

Over the years, we've helped hundreds of players secure positions in professional clubs and college teams across the country. Our alumni network includes players who have gone on to compete at national and international levels.

Our Facilities

We pride ourselves on having some of the best training facilities in the region, including:

  • Two full-sized professional football pitches
  • One indoor training facility for all-weather training
  • Strength and conditioning gym
  • Video analysis room
  • Sports medicine and recovery center

Our History

Assailant Fighters Academy was founded in 2019 by a group of former professional footballers and coaches who shared a common vision: to create a football academy that would develop players holistically, focusing on technical, tactical, physical, and mental aspects of the game.

What started as a small training group with just 15 players has now grown into one of the region's most respected football academies, with over 200 players across various age groups and programs.

Our milestone achievements include:

  • 2019: Academy founded with 15 players
  • 2020: First regional tournament victory
  • 2021: Expanded to include goalkeeper specialization program
  • 2022: 5 players signed with professional clubs
  • 2023: Opened new indoor training facility
  • 2024: 12 players received college football scholarships
