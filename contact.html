Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you accept?

We accept players from ages 6 to 18. Our programs are divided into age-appropriate groups: Youth Development (6-12), Advanced Training (13-18), and specialized programs for goalkeepers and elite players.

What is the training schedule?

Training schedules vary by program. Youth programs typically run 6 days a week for 3 hours per session. Advanced programs train 3-4 days a week for 2 hours per session. Specific schedules are provided upon registration.

Do you offer trial sessions?

Yes, we offer one free trial session for prospective players. This allows you to experience our training methodology and helps us assess the player's current level to recommend the appropriate program.

What should players bring to training?

Players should bring: football boots (for grass) and indoor shoes (for artificial turf), shin guards, water bottle, and appropriate training clothing for the weather conditions. All other training equipment is provided by the academy.
