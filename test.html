Assailant Fighters Edge Past Babon Gaw Yang Star

Sat, Oct 04 — Match report · Assailant Fighters Football Academy

A brilliantly delivered corner and a stunning finish from Mahid in the 30+3 minute clinched a 1–0 victory for Assailant Fighters in a tightly fought encounter.

Assailant Fighters Football Academy secured a well-earned 1–0 win over Babon Gaw Yang Star after Mahid rose to meet a set-piece and produced an excellent finish at 30+3 minutes. Both sides began in a 4-4-2 formation and the first half was characterised by strong defensive work and careful midfield battles, with Ornob, Ahsan, Tashrif and Majharul keeping the backline compact while Kawser and Al Amin orchestrated play from midfield. The decisive moment came from a corner where Mahid's poise and technique produced the only goal of the match.

In the second half, Babon Gaw Yang Star pushed for an equaliser, but Assailant Fighters managed the game intelligently — introducing Rumel, Pappu, Rasel and Shamim to refresh the attack and shore up possession. Goalkeeper Rabbi produced several important saves to protect the lead, and Coach Shumel’s tactical substitutions helped preserve the clean sheet. The team’s disciplined defending and timely attacking moments ensured three points, giving them positive momentum for the rest of the season.

Final score: Assailant Fighters 1–0 Babon Gaw Yang Star. Goal: Mahid (30+3'). Formations: both teams used 4–4–2. Man of the match: Mahid for the decisive contribution.
